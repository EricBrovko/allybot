Never forget the valuable moments in your work life Get it all in one place
What is that Wowly is a bot that helps you save the most valuable moments in your work life and shows them on a brilliant team’s memory board
How it works

Add Wowly to Slack Invite Wowly to the groups
channel or use private chat in
order to save the moments

Save moments that matter Use emoji or slash command
to save the moments you want.
Wowly will also save the most
popular moment during the day

See it all on one board Everyone can contribute to the
memories board by adding,
editing, deleting the moments

Why we built that People spend most of the time at work and during that time they always have some valuable moments. They’re learning, achieving, creating, having fun, however it’s easy to forget all of that
